Transformation and Digital Service Categories

Gold Services

A service is considered to belong to the GOLD category if it satisfies at least one of the following statements:

Any system that is considered critical for DEACERO by supporting Make, Move, Sell, Pay processes.
The disruption or any loss of data associated with this system could cause a big economical impact, or legal implications.
The system is being used in several locations.
The system needs to work 24/7.

Silver Services

A service is considered to belong to the SILVER category if it satisfies at least one of the following statements:

Any system whose disruption or loss of data would immediately cause a significant impact.
The system is used in only one location.
It is necessary to ensure that the system operates within normal office hours (M-F 8:30 to 6:30 pm) and is expected to be available outside of normal business hours.

Bronze Services

A service is considered to belong to the BRONZE category if it satisfies at least one of the following statements:

Any system whose disruption or loss of data would cause an impact, but not significant or immediate.
The system is used in only one location.
It is necessary to ensure that the system operates within normal office hours (M-F 8:30 to 6:30 pm) and is not expected to be available outside of normal business hours.
